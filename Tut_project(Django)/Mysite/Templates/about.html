ABOUT US

Welcome to LUXE-CEREUS, where passion meets craftsmanship. Our story began with a simple idea: to create high-quality, beautifully scented candles that bring warmth, light, and a touch of magic to every moment.

WHO WE ARE

We are a team of candle enthusiasts, artisans, and dreamers dedicated to making your home feel like a sanctuary. Based in a semi-urban region like Bokaro steel city, we hand-pour every jar candle with care, using only the finest ingredients and sustainable practices.

OUR CANDLES

Each candle is a blend of premium natural waxes, infused with thoughtfully curated fragrances designed to evoke emotions, create memories, and enhance your space. From soothing lavender to invigorating citrus, our scents are crafted to suit every mood and occasion.

WHAT SETS US APART

- Eco-Friendly Approach: We prioritize sustainability by using reusable jars, clean-burning wicks, and eco-conscious packaging.
- Handcrafted Excellence: Every candle is meticulously hand-poured, ensuring the highest level of quality and attention to detail.
- Personal Touch: We believe in creating products that tell a story, adding a personal touch to your special moments.
